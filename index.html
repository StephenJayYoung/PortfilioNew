About


My Coding Roots: I coded Every.Single.Day for the last year, and since then have been creating a new website every two weeks -- and I haven't even come close to getting sick of it. I got my start at Portland Code School, and since then, I've taken a number of classes, read nerdy books, worked on projects for various clients, and taught students the ways of JavaScript and other languages, tools and frameworks.

My Mission: My goal as a developer (and as a person) is to help build meaningful communities - so that the people involved in them can feel more fulfilled, be more productive, and become happier human beings. Check out my resume below to get a feel for how I've gone about doing this, or click on the links above to view some of the projects I've been working on!
